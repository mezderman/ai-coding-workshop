# Slack message from Dana Okafor

**#product** · Dana Okafor

Streaks + stats are both live and I've been watching the numbers — day-10 retention ticked up a bit but it's not the jump we needed. Feels like people check in, see the streak, but there's still not a lot pulling them back once a streak breaks. I keep coming back to the other half of the original idea: points, levels, the whole game-y layer.

Thinking something like — every check-in earns you points, maybe a bit of a bonus if you're on a longer streak, and those points roll up into a level, so there's a bigger number to watch grow than just a single habit's streak. And then badges on top for the fun milestone moments — first check-in, 7-day streak, 30-day streak, hit level 5, that kind of thing. Doesn't need to be a huge catalog to start, just enough that unlocking one feels like something.

Honestly not sure how deep to go on any of these — how many levels, what the point curve looks like, which badges matter, whether badges should be tied to streaks or points or both. Same deal as before though: scrappy first pass, see if the whole thing actually gets people coming back before we go build a huge system out of it. Ping me if you want to talk through any of it.

— Dana
